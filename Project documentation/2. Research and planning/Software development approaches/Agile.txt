An Agile software development approach emphasises on iterative development following a series of steps, collaboration with others and being
adaptable to changing requirements.

It divides work down into various phases, focusing on the continous delivery of development and furthermore improvement. It's the ability
to create and then additionally react and respond to changes as they happen and are required. 

It's broken down into six key steps, being concept, inception, iteration and construction, release, production and retirement.
